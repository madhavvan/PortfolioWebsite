Autply

Revolutionizing Communication with AI-Driven Messaging Solutions

Introduction

Autply is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to enhance your messaging experience by seamlessly integrating AI-driven solutions into daily communication. With Autply, intelligent conversations are just a tap away.

Core Functionality

Autply intelligently listens to incoming message notifications, processes their content, and instantly generates relevant AI-driven response suggestions. Using state-of-the-art natural language processing, Autply ensures every suggested reply aligns contextually with the received message.

  • Notification Listener: Automatically detects and reads incoming messages from WhatsApp.
  • AI-Powered Reply Suggestions: Generates meaningful replies tailored to the conversation.
  • Instant Access: Displays replies through a user-friendly pop-up notification for quick action.

Technical Excellence

Autply is built on a robust technical foundation to ensure high performance and reliability:

  • Kotlin: Optimized for Android with clean and maintainable code.
  • Jetpack Compose: For building a dynamic, user-friendly UI.
  • ChatGPT API: For generating intelligent and contextually relevant replies.
  • Accessibility Services: Enables interaction with WhatsApp notifications and automates response suggestions.
  • OkHttp: Facilitates seamless communication with the ChatGPT API.

Streamlined User Experience

Autply focuses on making messaging effortless by providing users with automated message handling and non-intrusive quick-action pop-ups.

Future Roadmap

  • Advanced customization for AI-generated replies.
  • Group chat compatibility.
