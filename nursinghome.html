Nursing Home Staffing & Quality Analysis

Poor quality of care in nursing homes has been a long-standing concern. Our study examines the link between staffing rating and overall rating based on health survey score results over a 3-year period.

We aim to determine if variables such as staffing rating, quality measure rating, and health survey rating have an influence on overall rating.

We employ quantitative research methods, using statistical analysis and machine learning techniques on nursing home datasets.

Our findings suggest a significant link between staffing quality and overall nursing home ratings, highlighting areas for improvement.
